>> Week 12 new feature
- The new implemented feature is a book rating.
- The scale is from 0-5 stars
- A short description of the rating will appear for every star:
    * 0 stars = blank
    * 1 star = bad
    * 2 stars = not good
    * 3 stars = average
    * 4 stars = good
    * 5 stars = Very good

- this feature will also be included when user clicks on the FAB button
- a card view is updated with the book review rating
